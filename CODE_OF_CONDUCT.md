# ValGuide Code of Conduct

ValGuide is built for cultural institutions, contributors, operators, and visitors who may come from different languages, disciplines, countries, and levels of technical experience. Our project spaces should make it possible for people to collaborate without having to tolerate harassment, intimidation, or contempt.

This code applies to ValGuide repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, project chat, and any place where someone is clearly representing the ValGuide project.

## Our pledge

ValGuide should be a project people can approach without needing the loudest voice, the longest resume, or the most insider context in the room. We expect contributors and maintainers to help keep participation respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

That commitment includes people of every age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, and sexual identity or orientation.

## Expected behavior

Contributors are expected to:

* communicate respectfully, especially when disagreeing
* assume practical constraints before assuming bad intent
* keep criticism focused on the work, design, code, documentation, or decision
* make room for people with different backgrounds and levels of context
* give clear feedback without personal attacks
* accept maintainer decisions once a direction has been set
* be mindful that accessibility, localization, privacy, and institutional trust are core to this project

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in ValGuide spaces:

* harassment, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption
* personal insults, slurs, demeaning jokes, or hostile comments
* sexualized comments, imagery, or unwanted attention
* publishing private information without explicit permission
* repeated bad-faith arguments after maintainers have asked for a discussion to stop
* pressuring others to disclose private identity, health, employment, financial, or security information
* any conduct that would make professional collaboration unsafe or needlessly hostile

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions that violate this code. They may also limit or revoke a person's ability to participate in ValGuide spaces.

Enforcement should be proportionate to the situation. Depending on severity and history, maintainers may use private warnings, public moderation, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to support@valguide.com. Include links, screenshots, dates, usernames, and any other context that helps maintainers understand what happened.

Reports will be handled with care. ValGuide maintainers will avoid sharing reporter details beyond the people needed to review and respond to the incident, unless disclosure is required by law or necessary to prevent serious harm.

## Resolution

When reviewing a report, maintainers will consider the impact of the behavior, the safety of affected people, the available evidence, and the risk of recurrence. The goal is not performative punishment; it is to keep ValGuide spaces workable, respectful, and safe for collaboration.

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to stop immediately.

## Attribution
